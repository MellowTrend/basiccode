Hey! You made it through the basic tags! How about some pizzazz.

Remember our ubiquitous friend, the "p" tag? It can be displayed in whatever color, font, or size you choose.


Here's how we add colorful language:


<p style="font-family:Verdana; color: #ff0000" > I am text colored purple </p>





Let's take it up a notch.

<p style="font-size: 35px;" > I am text in font size 35 pixels </p>





There are also different "heading" tags ranging from h1 (largest) through h5 (smallest).


<h3 style="font-size: 50px;" > I am text displayed in the h3 tag. </h3>
